Athlete of the Week


Physical Education Expectations and Norms at PWS

Respect Others

  • Stay on your feet
  • Do your best
  • Wear athletic shoes

Respect Our Space

  • Listen when others are speaking (don't interrupt)
  • T.H.I.N.K before you speak (is what you are going to say: True, Helpful, Inspiring, Necessary and Kind)? If you can't think of something nice to say...think harder
  • Be a good sport (Humble winner and Gracious Learner)

Respect Yourself

  • Use equipment with care and wait for instructions on how to use it (don't touch equipment without permission)
  • Leave the space (gym or outdoors) better than how you found it [leave no trace principles]
